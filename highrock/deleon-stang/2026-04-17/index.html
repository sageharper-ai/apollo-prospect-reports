Executive Summary

DeLeon & Stang is a well-established CPA firm in Frederick, MD with a structurally solid website — schema and Open Graph markup on all 99 crawled pages, a healthy 589-URL sitemap, and no redirect chains. The foundation is sound. Surface-level on-page SEO has real room to move: 554 images missing alt text (71% of all images), 901 external links without nofollow, 29 titles running over 60 characters, and 24 pages with multiple H1 tags.

The Frederick County keyword landscape is narrow but honest. Hyper-local demand on the Core 3 is real — 260 SV/mo across "cpa frederick md" (140), "tax preparation frederick md" (90), and "accounting firm frederick md" (30). Semi-local and general-tier sibling queries add depth (combined PPC-equivalent ceiling ~$9K/mo; conservative Core-3 floor ~$3,900/mo). The biggest single lever is combining the alt-text cleanup with an organic push on those three Core-3 keywords — both are this-quarter moves, not campaigns.
